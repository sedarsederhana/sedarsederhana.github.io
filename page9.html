Sisal Soap Net

Sisal comes from agave sisalana plant and is stronger and more durable than other natural fibers. This drawstring soap saver is great for those little leftover bits and pieces of soap, or a whole bar. It provides gentle exfoliation and rich lather to keep skin sparkly and clean. Can also be used together with soap nuts for laundry washing.

Directions for use:

To use in the shower, simply place your favorite soap bar in the sack, runs it through water and gently rub to your skin. Wring out excess water and hang to dry between uses.

Measurement: 13 cm L x 9 cm W
