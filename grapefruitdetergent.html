GRAPEFRUIT DETERGENT

Environmental friendly, concentrated and 100% biodegradable mixture that smells heavenly! Great for laundry washing and can be used as rug deodorizer.

Ingredients: Sodium Percarbonate, Sodium Bicarbonate, Sodium Carbonate, Sodium Chloride, Sodium Hydroxide, coconut oil, water and grapefruit oil Free from SLS / SLES/ ALS, 1,4-doixane, nonylphenol ethoxylate (NPE), phosphates, artificial fragrance and borax.

Directions for use:

Hand wash: ½ scoop

Washing machine:

5-7kg: 1 scoop

7-9kg: 1 ½ scoop

9-12kg: 2 scoops

*Capacity of each scoop is about 30gm

To use as rug deodorizer, sprinkle a good amount on rug, sweep or brush through evenly, leave it for 30 minutes and vacuum
