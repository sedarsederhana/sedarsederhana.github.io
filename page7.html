Potato Soap

Potato juice is a host of nutrients including vitamin B and C, potassium, calcium, iron, phosphorus and copper. With dense lather, this plant based soap has excellent cleaning power! It is suitable to use for general cleaning, remove stain, dishwashing and laundry. It is also moist and gentle to skin. 

Ingredients: Coconut oil, palm oil, filtered water and fresh potato juice 

Weight: ~90g
