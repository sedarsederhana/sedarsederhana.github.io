SOAP NUT BERRIES

Comes from Sapindus Mukorossi tree, soap nuts are ‘magic berries’ that are used as environmental friendly alternative to chemical detergent and cleaners. The shell absorbs water and releases saponins which circulate as a natural surfactant freeing dirt, grime and oils from clothing. These nuts are amazingly versatile as they can be further made into liquid cleaner as body wash, shampoo, dish wash, floor, glass and multipurpose cleaner. They are gentle on clothes and skin, suitable for those with sensitive skin, including babies, pets and those that suffer from allergies, eczema and psoriasis.  

Directions for use:

To use as laundry detergent, simply put 6-7 nuts in a drawstring pouch for full load washing. The nuts can be reused up to 8 times of full load washing.

To make liquid soap, simply boil a handful of soap nuts and a cup of water for 10 minutes.
